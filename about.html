About

Hi, I'm Harsahibjit Singh, the Webmaster of IEEE CUSB. This website was created to simplify the work of our student branch executives by centralizing tasks like event management, communication, and resource access, making their responsibilities more efficient. A special thanks to Er. Sugandha Sharma, our Branch Counsellor, whose guidance has helped shape this platform into a tool that empowers our members. Whether you're here to stay updated, explore our team's work, or contribute ideas, this website is your gateway to IEEE CUSB.

Our Mission

Empowering students to explore, innovate, and lead in the ever-evolving world of technology. Together, we aim to shape the future through knowledge, creativity, and collaboration. At IEEE CUSB, we aim to empower students to dream big and achieve bigger through technical excellence, innovation, and collaboration.
